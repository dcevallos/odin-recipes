Lemon Pie

This sour cream lemon pie is a favorite at our family gatherings. Top each slice with a dollop of whipped cream if you like. 1/4 cup cornstarch may be substituted for flour.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups milk
  • 3 egg yolks
  • ¼ cup butter
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • 1 ½ teaspoons lemon zest
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked

Steps

  1. Combine sugar, flour, and salt in a saucepan. Gradually stir in milk.
  2. Cook and stir on medium heat until thickened and bubbly.
  3. Reduce heat, cook, and stir for 2 more minutes. Remove from heat.
  4. Beat yolks slightly.
  5. Gradually stir 1 cup of custard mix into yolks.
  6. Return yolk mixture to the saucepan with the rest of custard and bring to a gentle boil.
  7. Cook and stir for 2 more minutes.
  8. Remove from heat and stir in butter, lemon juice, and zest. Fold in sour cream.
  9. Pour filling into baked pie shell. Chill until set.
